## July 2026

### v2.3.0 \u2014 Evidence management

- `PATCH /evidence/:id/tags` \u2014 Tag evidence with searchable labels
- `POST /evidence/:id/review` \u2014 Approve or decline evidence with reviewer notes
- `POST /evidence/public-upload/proxy-upload` \u2014 Browser-safe upload (no CORS)

### v2.2.0 \u2014 Voice intelligence

- Default scenario changed to `customerSupportComplaint` for intake/FNOL calls
- Auto-retry on `SCENARIO_MISMATCH_NOT_SCORED` with the API-suggested scenario
- Enriched results: `headline`, `recommendedDecision`, `executiveSummary`, `keyScores`, `importantCallSignals`

### v2.1.0 \u2014 Forensics

- `POST /forensics/poi-fraud-check` \u2014 Composite fraud score combining voice, KYC, and evidence per participant

### v2.0.0 \u2014 Audit logging

- Global interceptor logs every mutating request with actor name, email, IP, and timing
- `GET /audit/logs` supports `sortBy`, `sortOrder`, `page`, `limit`, `resourceType`, `resourceId` filters

## June 2026

### v1.9.0 \u2014 Organisation KYC

- `POST /organizations/:id/run-kyc` \u2014 Business verification with automatic director participant creation

### v1.8.0 \u2014 Claims workflow

- Assigning an investigator auto-transitions claim to `INVESTIGATING`
- New investigation fields: `partiesContacted`, `riskIndicators`, `evidenceReviewed`

### v1.7.0 \u2014 Sanctions screening

- `POST /participants/sanctions-sources/sync` \u2014 public endpoint to refresh watchlist sources
- `organizationId` now inferred server-side from JWT context
