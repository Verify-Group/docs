---
title: Organisations
description: Verify Group is multi-tenant, every claim, participant, and KYC request is scoped to an organisation identified by the X-Organization-ID header.
---

Every resource (claims, participants, KYC requests) belongs to an **Organisation**. Pass your UUID in every request:

```
X-Organization-ID: cfa70c25-1800-4748-97b0-ce675940a537
```

## Run KYC for your organisation

```bash
POST /api/v1/organizations/{id}/run-kyc
```
```json
{"autoSync": true}
```

Automatically creates a business participant, runs `business_ubo` verification, and generates participant records for each director.
