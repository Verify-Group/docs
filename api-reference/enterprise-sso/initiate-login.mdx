---
title: "Initiate SAML Login"
api: "GET /api/v1/auth/saml/{orgId}/login"
description: "Redirects the end-user to the configured Identity Provider login page. Use as your SSO login button URL."
authMethod: "none"
---

<ParamField path="orgId" type="string" required>
  Your Organisation UUID.
</ParamField>

Responds with `302 Found` redirecting to the IdP SSO URL with a signed SAML `AuthnRequest`.
